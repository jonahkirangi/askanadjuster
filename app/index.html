What We Do

We are not attorneys. We are not lawyers. We are insurance adjusters. The very same insurance adjusters you speak with when you're involved in an auto accident. Need help understanding the claims process? We're here to answer questions and give you detailed insider information about how to settle your claim - all without charging you the typical 33% attorney fee.

Map The Process

Not knowing what to expect can be the most frustrating part of an auto insurance claim. Our goal is to make sure that you know what your adjuster will say and when they will say it. Have a unique question? This is the best place to get an answer.

Explain Your Options

Do I have to accept the insurance company's offer? Why aren't they covering all of the damage on my car? Should I file a claim with my own insurance company? We'll discuss your options in order to help you make the right choices for your settlement.

Protect Your Privacy

All correspondence is secure and private. The details of your case will remain strictly confidential and will not be shared with any individuals or third-parties. Ever. Your privacy and trust come first.

How It Works

A sample walk through of a common rear-end collision claim

  • January 1

    Rear-end impact occurs

    You stopped for the red light. The driver behind you didn't. Your car has damage. Your neck hurts. The police are called and a report is filed. Luckily, your car is drivable and you are able to make it home. You spend the evening resting, not sure what to expect or who you'll be hearing from over the next few days.

  • January 4

    The at-fault insurance company calls

    They accept fault and offer to fix your car. You choose to go to their service facility for your estimate and check. You advise them of your sore neck and they state that an injury specialist adjuster will call you back within a day or so.

  • January 5

    You contact AskAnAdjuster.com

    You receive guidance on what questons to ask during the inspection and estimate of your car. You also receive information about how to obtain a rental car during repairs and how to claim damage to personal property inside your car. You receive guidance on what to expect and what questions to ask during your upcoming conversation with the injury adjuster.

  • January 14

    You settle your injury claim

    With the information provided to you by AskAnAdjuster.com, you were able to negotiate a higher settlement. You were also able to negotiate full compensation for your pain, in addition to your lost wages and out of pocket expenses.
